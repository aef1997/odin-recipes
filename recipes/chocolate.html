Easy Chocolate

Description

Easy vegan hot chocolate to keep you warm during the winter!

Ingredients
  • 1 cup water
  • ¼ cup white sugar
  • ¼ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 3 cups soy milk
Steps
  1. Bring water, sugar, and cocoa to a boil in a small saucepan
  2. Pour in soy milk and stir until combined and heated through, about 3 minutes.
